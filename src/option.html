Q: How to open this page ?

A: Right click the extension icon on the top right, and select `optoins`.

Q: How to enable scanner function?

A: Open chrome://flags/#enable-experimental-web-platform-features in Chrome, and enable it, and then relaunch Chrome.（ BTW: This function may not totally support on your Chrome Browser.）
